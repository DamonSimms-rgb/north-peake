About Us

Practical Health & Safety Advice That Protects Your Business

At North Peake Health & Safety Consultants, we believe that effective health and safety should support your business—not slow it down.

We provide practical, proportionate and commercially focused health and safety consultancy, helping organisations meet their legal obligations while creating safer, healthier workplaces for employees, contractors and visitors.

With extensive experience working across construction, engineering, manufacturing, industrial and commercial environments, we understand the real-world challenges businesses face every day. Our advice is based on practical experience rather than simply quoting legislation, allowing us to deliver solutions that are realistic, effective and tailored to your organisation.

Whether you require a one-off inspection, ongoing competent health and safety support, risk assessments or assistance achieving recognised standards such as ISO 45001, we are committed to delivering professional, reliable and responsive services.
