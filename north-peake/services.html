Our Services

Supporting Your Business with Practical Health & Safety Solutions

North Peake Health & Safety Consultants provide a comprehensive range of health and safety services designed to support businesses across a variety of industries.

Health & Safety Consultancy

Professional advice and ongoing competent person support to help your business meet its legal duties and maintain high standards of health and safety.

Risk Assessments

Preparation and review of suitable and sufficient risk assessments covering all aspects of your operations.

Method Statements (RAMS)

Development and review of Risk Assessments and Method Statements that are practical, compliant and easy for your workforce to follow.

Site Inspections & Audits

Regular workplace inspections and detailed health and safety audits to identify risks, monitor compliance and recommend improvements.

Construction Health & Safety

Support for contractors, principal contractors, developers and clients including construction site inspections, CDM compliance, contractor management, construction phase documentation and health & safety plans.

ISO 45001 Support

Guidance for organisations looking to implement, improve or maintain an effective Occupational Health & Safety Management System in line with ISO 45001.

Accident & Incident Investigation

Professional investigations into workplace incidents, identifying root causes and practical corrective actions to reduce the likelihood of recurrence.

Policies & Procedures

Development and review of company health and safety policies, procedures and management systems tailored to your organisation.

Fire Safety

Support with fire safety management, fire risk assessments and emergency procedures.

Training & Awareness

Health and safety training, toolbox talks and awareness sessions designed to improve competence and promote a positive safety culture.

Ongoing Retained Support

Flexible retained consultancy packages providing ongoing advice, documentation reviews and regular support whenever your business needs it.

No matter the size of your organisation, our goal is to provide practical advice that keeps your people safe while supporting your business objectives.
